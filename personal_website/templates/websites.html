Vom Rugerhaus German Shepherds

Intermediate

A small-business website for a German Shepherd breeder, focused on clear branding, kennel presentation, and upcoming litter promotion. The layout is designed to make it easy for potential buyers to quickly see dogs, learn about the kennel, and find contact information.

This project helped me practice turning real client needs into a structured layout: hero sections, about content, litter info, photo areas, and contact calls-to-action. It also reinforced responsive design so the site stays usable on phones and tablets where most visitors browse.
